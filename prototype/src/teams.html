Helsinki REDS

TThe original Helsinki REDS Overwatch team was assembled by team manager Christer Kasurinen and the first team captain Roope “Ruape” Kurkela. During the fall of 2016, they gathered a tight-knit group of players committed to working hard and growing together to become a team that is competitive in Overwatch on an international level. In the fall of 2017, the Helsinki REDS announced an expansion into Dota 2 with our first roster for the game.

Detroit Renegades

The Renegades, also known as the Detroit Renegades and previously LA Renegades, is a professional eSports organization that fields teams in Counter-Strike: Global Offensive, Overwatch, Paladins, Rocket League, Vainglory, and Super Smash Bros.
